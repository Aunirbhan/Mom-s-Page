Rina's Story

This page was created for my mother, Rina—a strong and beautiful woman born in Bangladesh, carrying countless stories from her childhood in a vibrant and bountiful country.

In pursuit of her dream to start a family in America, she immigrated to New York City with her husband Debasish, where together they raised two sons Aurnab and Aunirbhan and took on every burden they could if it meant giving their sons the opportunity to succeed.

Though the journey was never easy, their sacrifices allowed me to grow up, be educated, and chase my ambitions in the best city in the world. I am endlessly proud of my parents and deeply grateful for the life they worked so hard to build.

Now, I am using the coding skills I’ve begun developing in school to help my mom pursue the dreams she once had to put aside while supporting mine. Help her fund her dreams through the links above.

This page exists to share her story, help her stay connected, and allow those who believe in her journey to offer support. Feel free to reach out.
